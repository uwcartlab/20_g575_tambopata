Brazil Nut Harvester

Maria Silva has harvested Brazil nuts since she was a child. Her family holds a 40-year concession for harvesting Brazil nuts in 500 hectares of old growth forest in the northeastern part of the Tambopata National Reserve.

Wildlife Ecologist

Dr. Natalia Ortiz is a Peruvian scientist who studies the ecology of Giant River Otters (Pteronura brasiliensis) in Tambopata, one of the last strongholds for this endangered species.

Small-Scale Gold Miner

Pedro Gomez is a long-time resident of Tambopata and a gold miner who has been mining the banks of the Malinowski river for 20 years.
